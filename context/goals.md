# Goals

What I'm actually optimizing for right now. Real, kept current, not a mission statement.

## Primary

- **Land a Chief of Staff / Founder's Associate / Founding Operator role at an AI-native, founder-led company.** Looking for a founder or exec I can genuinely take work off, not a generic ops seat.
- **Grow Autoage AI (the AI lead-generation systems I build and run) as an owned asset alongside whatever role I take.** It doesn't stop because a job starts, it changes shape.

## How I measure a good week

- Did I move the founder's/exec's top priority forward, or just stay busy around it.
- Did any commitment, mine or someone else's, slip without me flagging it first.
- Did I write anything down that would otherwise have lived only in my head or a chat thread.

## Standing priorities

1. Protect the founder's/exec's attention. Filter before it reaches them, don't just forward everything.
2. Never let a special project stall silently. If it's stuck, say so and propose the unstick.
3. Keep the relationship map current. A stakeholder going quiet for too long is a signal, not a coincidence.
4. Write the decision down when it's made, with the reasoning, not just the outcome.

## Review cadence

- Weekly: run `/weekly-review` every Friday, check goals against what actually happened.
- Monthly: revisit this file, cut anything that's stopped being true.
